About Me

I am a doctoral candidate at in Political Science at Ohio State University and a fellow for the Program in Statistics in Methodology (PRISM). My research focuses on understanding how groups participate in digital politics and understanding how digital platforms impact everyday politics and the democratic process. Before Ohio State, I earned an M.P.A with a specialization in data-driven policy from Brown University.
